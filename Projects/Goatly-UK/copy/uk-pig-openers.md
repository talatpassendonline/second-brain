> [!note] Wat dit is
> P.I.G.-stijl openers (Chris Haddad, kennisbank playbook 09) voor de UK long-form natives en de advertorial. Doel: de huidige zachte openingen omzetten naar een visceraal Moment of Crisis met een open loop en nul benefit-beloftes vóór de vouw. Compliance = [[me|Talat]] (rule 16): de crisis komt uit echte VoC ([[uk-macro-avatars]]), geen ziekte-/genezingsclaims.

## De motor (uit het swipe-voorbeeld)

Talat's voorbeeld: *"I Caught My Husband With His Hand Down His Pants At 1 AM Hunched Over His Phone... And What He Whispered Next Made Me Question Everything I Thought I Knew About MEN."*

Vijf onderdelen die we kopiëren:
1. **In-medias-res scene**, concreet en zintuiglijk: tijd (1 AM), houding (hunched), handeling. Geen abstractie.
2. **Open loop**: "what he whispered next". Je MOET het weten.
3. **Identity-reframe**: "everything I thought I knew about MEN". Zoomt van één moment naar een wereldbeeld.
4. **Nul benefit-belofte.** Verkoopt niets. Pure emotie.
5. **First person + lichte taboe/schaamte-rand.**

## Headline-swipe (zelfde motor, per avatar)

**Vanishing Frame (becoming my mother):**
- "My Three-Year-Old Granddaughter Lifted Her Arms for Me to Carry Her Up to Bed, the Way I Always Had. I Heard Myself Say No. And I Finally Understood the Thing I'd Been Hiding From for Two Years."
- "My Daughter Hugged Me Goodbye at the Airport. Her Chin Rested on the Top of My Head. She Pulled Back, Looked at Me, and Asked the One Question I Haven't Been Able to Unhear Since."

**Betrayed Pill-Taker:**
- "For Nineteen Years I Took My Calcium Every Single Morning. Then a Pharmacist Leaned Across the Counter, Lowered Her Voice, and Told Me to Stop. What She Said Next Made Me Furious."
- "The Nurse Read My Scan Twice. Then She Asked if I'd Been Taking Anything for My Bones. I Had. Every Day for Twenty Years. The Silence After I Answered Said Everything."

**Independence Guardian:**
- "She Cut My Food Into Small Pieces and Slid the Plate Back to Me. The Way I Used to Cut Hers. I Was Sixty-One, and I Said Thank You, and I Have Never Felt Smaller."

## Voor / na: opening van becoming-my-mother

**NU (huidige opening):** headline *"I kept telling myself it was just getting older. Then my daughter stood next to me."* De echte gut-punch ("Mum, when did you get so small?") staat pas in alinea 3. De granddaughter-op-de-trap, de sterkste scene, staat in alinea ~13.

**P.I.G. (Moment of Crisis vooraan, beat map):**

> My three-year-old granddaughter lifted her arms for me to carry her up to bed, the way I always had. I heard myself say no. And in that moment I finally understood the thing I had been hiding from for two years.
>
> She was happy again in a second. Grandma will walk up beside you instead. She skipped up holding the bannister and never gave it another thought.
>
> I stood at the bottom of those stairs and could not move.
>
> Because the "no" was never about the stairs. It was the hundred small noes before it that I had never let myself count. The cardigan that suddenly sat wrong. The trousers dragging on the floor that I kept taking up, blaming the shop. The word I had started whispering to myself a dozen times a day. Careful. Careful on the wet path. Careful lifting her.
>
> I was 56, and I had decided, without ever deciding, that this was simply what 56 was.
>
> It was not. And the thing it actually was, I had already watched it happen once. To my mother. Who did everything the doctors ever told her, every single day, and folded in on herself anyway.

Nul benefit-belofte, opent op de crisis, benoemt de villain (het stille ding), zet een dead end (moeder deed alles goed en het gebeurde toch), en houdt de loop open richting "wat zei de verpleegkundige". Mechanisme (food not pills) komt pas ná de proof, als "Secrets and Magic".

## Compliance

Crisis-beelden komen uit geverifieerde VoC (granddaughter/stairs, cardigan, "careful", moeder die invouwt). Geen DEXA-, ziekte- of genezingsclaim in de hook. Talat beoordeelt.

## Congruentie-guardrail (DR-principe, niet onderhandelbaar)

De gut-punch P.I.G. Line verhoogt je CTR. Maar een hoge CTR zonder congruentie brandt geld (de "spend maar geen conversie"-les, kennisbank playbook 06 §1b). **Regel: de P.I.G. Line moet dezelfde diagnose en hetzelfde mechanisme beloven als de advertorial waar hij heen linkt.** Een viscerale hook is alleen winst als de landing 'm waarmaakt.
