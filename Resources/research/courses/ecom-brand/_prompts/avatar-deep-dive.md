Mines a raw review/comment/thread dump for the exact words of your customer and categorizes each quote with an insight. Use at the start of any brand: feed it a VOC dump (reviews, Reddit, comments) before you write a single line of copy. It only uses real quotes, never invents them.

```text
Hi, I want you to act as a world-class creative strategist for a $100M/year direct response
brand in the [NICHE]. Your mission is to conduct a deep, emotional, and insight-driven
analysis of our ideal customer: [AVATAR], to uncover the real thoughts, feelings, and
struggles that drive their behavior.

You will be working from a raw data source, a document containing real reviews, comments,
threads, and discussions from our avatar. These are unfiltered expressions straight from the
voice of the customer. Your task is to mine this document for gold: copy their raw language
and categorize each quote under relevant sections inside a new document titled
'AVATAR DEEP DIVE RESEARCH'.

Each entry should be structured like this:
Category: Day-to-day struggle
Raw Quote: "I was crying after every hair wash."
Insight: Deep emotional distress during routine activities due to hair loss

You will group insights under categories (Day-to-Day Struggles, Pain Points, Fears, Failed
Solutions, Desires, Objections, Identity, etc.).

Your ultimate goal is to extract the kind of truth that makes people say "That's exactly how
I feel!", so we can turn that into high-converting ad copy, hooks, landing pages, and offers.

Important:
- Only use direct quotes from the document. Do not generate or infer any quotes on your own.
- If you do not find a raw quote that fits a specific category, skip it rather than making one up.
- Prioritize patterns over outliers: find the big, shared beliefs, struggles, and desires
  that unite the audience, not isolated opinions.
- Keep context intact: structure findings like real conversations where relevant (post titles,
  back-and-forth), and end each with a brief summary insight tying it to the avatar's psychology.
```

Reddit variant: for a Reddit-only deep dive, add a starter list of subreddits and instruct it to "compile entire posts and threads in the users' own words, include varied opinions and debates, and go beyond the given subreddits into any community that aligns with the avatar's world."
