# Services / Offer

Single SKU (Goatly Bot Routine geitenmelkpoeder), three bundles, plus e-book gifts that ladder with bundle size.

## Bundles & prices

| Bundle | Inhoud | Prijs | Per zak |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 zak | 1 zak | €39,95 | €39,95 |
| **2+1** | 3 zakken + 2 e-books | €69,95 | €23,32 |
| **3+2** | 5 zakken + 3 e-books | €109,95 | €21,99 |

## Guarantee

**90 dagen geld-terug-garantie, ook bij lege zak.** Until reviews exist, this is the strongest trust signal we have (see [[icp]] and [[brand]] for the proof-gap).

## Scarcity

Real. Production runs in batches. "Elke batch is op" is honest framing, not a tactic. Compliance hard rail: never fake scarcity.

## Bonus stack (e-books)

- 2+1 → 2 e-books
- 3+2 → 3 e-books

Use as a value-anchor in offer copy. **Open item:** e-book titles + content not yet documented. Needed to fill the value-stack and feed email flows.

## Funnel

- **Cold traffic → advertorial** (long-form). Full mechanism + proof-stack. Advertorial URL: https://trygoatly.nl/pages/adv2-nieuws-botten
- **Warm + retargeting → PDP**. Offer-first creatives win here. PDP URL: https://trygoatly.nl/products/goatly-bot-routine (Kaching Bundles for bundle select)
- **Congruentie verplicht:** ad and advertorial share the same diagnosis + mechanism.

## Current AOV

**€67,22 tracked** (~at the 2+1 bundle price of €69,95). Most buyers already take a bundle. The lever now isn't fixing single-pouch buyers. It's pushing 3+2 and adding subscription / refill. See [[strategy]] for the AOV test plan.

## Open data gaps

- COGS / margin per bundle → needed for break-even ROAS.
- Zakgrootte / dagen per zak → needed for "kost per dag"-framing and refill timing.
- E-book titles + content → needed for value-stack copy.
