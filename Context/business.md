# Goatly

## What it is

## Why it works (the mechanism in one breath)

A calcium pill is calciumcarbonaat = vermalen kalk. Body absorbs ~20–25%. Goatly delivers calcium *with* its natural bouwteam: fosfor (1.2:1), magnesium, vitamine K2 (the wegwijzer), A2-eiwit. One schep (~10g) ≈ 400mg calcium + A2. See [[brand]] for the full wedge and how to talk about it.
