## Unclosed Loops (the meta-problem)

The drain isn't a specific workflow. It's **idea-vanishing**.

Idea pops up → jot it down somewhere, sometimes discuss with Claude, sometimes start work. If time runs out or I have to leave → it vanishes in the dust. By the time I come back to it, it's too late.

**This vault exists to catch those loops.** That means:
- Every idea / loop / decision-to-make goes into `Daily/YYYY-MM-DD.md` or the right project folder when raised.
- Weekly review surfaces open loops → do / park / kill.
- Claude proactively flags loops sitting unmade.
